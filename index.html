About us


We are a group of good friends, trying to make you laugh and have great time with us.

Our organisation was created in 2017 and since than, we have succesfully gained new players and made good placements in several tournaments.

We succesfully managed to play in 2019 WorldCup qualifiers and win over 2600$.

Right now, we are mainly focusing on consistent placements in CS:GO and Fortnite tournaments.

We are also open to new players and opportunities, so feel free to contact us!
